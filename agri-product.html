Topic 10

Product

Expanded discussion on product concepts in agricultural marketing.

Product

A product refers to anything that can be offered to the market to satisfy a need or want.

In agricultural marketing, products include raw agricultural goods as well as processed food items.

Types of Agricultural Products

1. Fresh Agricultural Products

These are unprocessed goods such as vegetables, fruits, grains, livestock, and fish.

2. Processed Agricultural Products

These are goods that have undergone processing such as canned goods, milk products, and dried foods.

Product Quality

Product quality refers to the ability of a product to meet consumer expectations and standards.

It is measured based on consumer satisfaction rather than the producer’s perception.

Indicators of Product Quality

Intrinsic Cues

Physical characteristics of the product such as taste, color, texture, and freshness.

Extrinsic Cues

External signals such as brand name, packaging, price, and advertising.

Importance of Product Decisions

Helps meet consumer needs effectively.

Increases market competitiveness.

Improves profitability and market acceptance.
