Cooperative Core Functions

1. Marketing

Cooperatives help extend control of members’ products through processing, distribution, and sale. This allows members to get better prices and wider market access for their goods.

2. Purchasing

Cooperatives provide affordable supplies and goods to members by buying in bulk and reducing costs. This helps members save money on production inputs and household needs.

3. Service

Cooperatives provide essential services needed by members such as financial services, training, credit access, and other support systems that improve productivity and welfare.
