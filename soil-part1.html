🌱 Part 1: Concept of Soil

Soil Definition

  • A mixture of organic and inorganic materials formed through weathering
  • Influenced by climate, organisms, topography, parent material, and time
  • Serves as a medium for plant growth
  • Provides water and nutrients
  • Considered a non-renewable resource

Functions of Soil

  • Supports plant roots (anchorage)
  • Stores and supplies water
  • Provides essential nutrients
  • Supports biological activity

Soil as a Natural Body

  • Has thickness and width
  • Has horizontal boundaries
  • Extends vertically from air to unweathered rock

Soil Individual

  • Pedon – smallest unit of soil (1–10 m²)
  • Polypedon – group of pedons forming a soil series

Approaches in Soil Study

  • Pedological – soil formation and classification
  • Edaphological – soil use for plant growth

Components of Soil

  • Mineral Matter (45%)
  • Organic Matter (5%)
  • Water (20–30%)
  • Air (20–30%)
Soil = 45% Mineral + 5% Organic + 25% Water + 25% Air
