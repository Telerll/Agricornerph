🌱 Concept of Crop Science

Crop Science is the study of plant growth, development, and management for food, fiber, and other agricultural purposes.

It focuses on:

    📚 Crop Science

    Study of plant growth, crop production, and management practices.

🌿 Plant Classification

  • Annual – completes life cycle in 1 season (rice, corn)
  • Biennial – 2 seasons (carrot)
  • Perennial – lives for many years (mango)

🌞 Photosynthesis

Plants produce food using sunlight.

Formula: CO₂ + H₂O + light → glucose + O₂

🌾 Cropping Systems

  • Monocropping – one crop only
  • Intercropping – multiple crops together
  • Crop Rotation – changing crops over time

🌱 Propagation

  • Sexual – seeds
  • Asexual – cuttings, grafting, budding
