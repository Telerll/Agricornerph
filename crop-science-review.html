Key concepts for ALE preparation

🌿 Plant Growth & Development

Includes germination, vegetative growth, flowering, and fruiting stages.

☀️ Photosynthesis

Process by which plants convert sunlight into energy (glucose).

🌾 Plant Propagation

Methods include sexual (seeds) and asexual (cuttings, grafting).

🌱 Soil-Plant Relationship

Soil provides nutrients, water, and anchorage for plant growth.
