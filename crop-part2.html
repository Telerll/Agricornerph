🌿 Crop Science

Part 2: Plant Morphology

🧬 A. Plant Cells

Cell → Basic unit of life that performs all plant functions.

🔹 Main Components

  • Cell wall → rigid outer layer for protection
  • Cytoplasm → fluid where organelles are found
  • Nucleus → control center containing DNA

🧱 Cell Wall (Layers)

  • Middle lamella → cements cells together
  • Primary wall → flexible wall during growth
  • Secondary wall → thick wall after growth

💧 Cytoplasm & Organelles

  • Plasma membrane → controls entry & exit of substances
  • Rough ER → protein production
  • Smooth ER → lipid production
  • Golgi apparatus → packaging & secretion
  • Mitochondria → energy production (ATP)

🌿 Plastids

  • Chloroplast → photosynthesis
  • Chromoplast → color pigments
  • Leucoplast → storage (starch, protein, fat)

🔬 Other Organelles

  • Ribosomes → protein synthesis
  • Vacuole → storage & waste removal
  • Cytoskeleton → cell support & movement
  • Microbodies → metabolic reactions

🌱 B. Plant Tissues

Meristematic Tissue

Meristematic Tissue → actively dividing cells for growth.

  • Apical → increases length
  • Lateral → increases thickness
  • Intercalary → regrowth (grass)

Permanent Tissue

Permanent Tissue → mature cells with specific function.

  • Parenchyma → storage & photosynthesis
  • Collenchyma → flexible support
  • Sclerenchyma → rigid support

Vascular Tissue

  • Xylem → transports water upward
  • Phloem → transports food

🌳 Plant Tissue System

  • Ground tissue → storage & photosynthesis
  • Dermal tissue → protection
  • Vascular tissue → transport system

🌿 C. Plant Organs

🌱 Root

Root → absorbs water and anchors plant.

  • Root cap → protects tip
  • Cell division → growth zone
  • Elongation → increases length
  • Maturation → forms root hairs

Types of Roots

  • Taproot → main deep root
  • Adventitious → grows from stem/leaves
  • Fibrous → many fine roots (monocots)
  • Foliar root → grows from leaves

🌿 Stem

Stem → supports plant and transports materials.

Types
  • Bulb → underground storage
  • Rhizome → horizontal stem
  • Runner → creeping stem
  • Tuber → swollen storage stem
  • Corm → solid underground stem
Forms
  • Erect → upright
  • Weak → creeping or climbing

🍃 Leaf

Leaf → main site of photosynthesis.

  • Leaf base → attaches to stem
  • Petiole → stalk
  • Blade → flat green surface
Venation
  • Reticulate → net-like
  • Parallel → straight lines

🌸 Flower

Flower → reproductive organ of plant.

  • Sepals → protect bud
  • Petals → attract pollinators
  • Stamen → male part
  • Pistil → female part

Complete flower → all parts present.

🌰 Seed

Seed → develops into a new plant.

  • Embryo → young plant
  • Cotyledon → food storage
  • Seed coat → protection
  • Plumule → shoot
  • Hypocotyl → stem part
Types
  • Monocot → one cotyledon
  • Dicot → two cotyledons

🌾 Fruits

Fruit → mature ovary containing seeds.

Types of Fruits
  • Simple → from one ovary
  • Aggregate → many ovaries, one flower
  • Multiple → many flowers
Fleshy Fruits
  • Berry → soft entire fruit
  • Hesperidium → citrus type
  • Pepo → thick rind
  • Drupe → hard seed inside
  • Pome → fleshy with core
Dry Fruits
  • Indehiscent → does not open
  • Dehiscent → splits open
