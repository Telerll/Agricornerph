Meaning of Extension in Other Countries

Netherlands

Term: Voorlichting

Meaning: Lighting the path ahead to help people find their way.

Indonesia

Term: Penyuluhan

Meaning: Lighting the way ahead with a torch.

Malaysia

Term: Perkembangan

Meaning: Education, the way USA interprets it.

Germany

Term: Beratung

Meaning: Advisory work where experts give guidance but final responsibility remains with the individual.

Germany

Term: Aufklärung

Meaning: Enlightenment so that one clearly knows where he or she is going.

Germany

Term: Erziehung

Meaning: Education that teaches people to solve their own problems.

Austria

Term: Förderung

Meaning: Furthering or stimulating people toward a desirable direction.

France

Term: Vulgarisation Animation

Meaning: Simplification of messages for the common people.

Spain

Term: Capacitacion Consultacion

Meaning: Improving people’s abilities through training.

USA

Term: Extension

Meaning: Education or influencing people to change their behavior.
