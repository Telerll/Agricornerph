Meaning of Extension in Other Countries

International Terms and Meanings of Extension

Term Country Meaning
Voorlichting Netherlands Lighting the path ahead to help people find their way
Penyuluhan Indonesia Lighting the way ahead with a torch
Perkembangan Malaysia Education, the way USA interprets it
Beratung Germany “Advisory work” — an expert can give advice on the best way to reach one’s goal but leaves the final responsibility for selecting the way
Aufklärung Germany “Enlightenment” so that one knows clearly where he/she is going
Erziehung Germany “Education,” that is, to teach people to solve their problems themselves
Förderung Austria “Furthering” or stimulating one to go in a desirable direction
Vulgarisation Animation France Simplification of the message for the common man
Capacitacion Consultacion Spain Improving people’s abilities, normally through training
Extension USA Education, or influencing people to change their behavior
