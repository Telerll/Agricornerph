🌱 Crop Science – Part 1

Introduction & Fundamentals of Crop Science

1. Agriculture

Concept of Agriculture

  • Systematic raising of useful plants and livestock under human management
  • Purposeful use of natural resources to produce food and other needs
  • Broad industry involving production, processing, marketing, and distribution

2. World & Domestic Food Situation

  • World population projected to reach 9.1 billion by 2050
  • Majority of growth will come from developing countries
  • Increasing pressure on agricultural land
  • Arable land is only about 10% of total land area
  • By 2050, available arable land may decrease to around 0.1 ha/person

Implication: Food production must be increased efficiently.

3. Philippine Agriculture Situation

  • Population (2009): 92.23 million
  • Growth rate: about 2.04%
  • Agricultural land: about 32%
  • Agriculture GDP share: about 18%
  • Growth supported by:
    • Irrigation
    • Crop protection
    • Improved varieties
    • Better nutrition
    • Postharvest technology

4. State of Philippine Agriculture

  • RA 8435 – Agriculture and Fisheries Modernization Act (AFMA)
  • Signed in 1997
  • Main goal: Modernize the agriculture and fisheries sector

5. Crop Science

  • Study of economically cultivated crops
  • Based on observation and scientific principles
  • Main goal: maximize crop production and benefits

6. Basic Terms

Plant

  • Member of Kingdom Plantae
  • Has no locomotion
  • Photosynthetic organism

Crop

  • Cultivated plant grown for profit or use

7. Branches of Crop Science

Agronomy

  • Deals with field crops and soil management

Horticulture

  • Deals with garden crops
  • Pomology – fruits
  • Olericulture – vegetables
  • Floriculture – flowers
  • Nursery management
  • Landscape gardening

8. Crop Production as Science, Art, and Business

Science

  • Based on chemistry, physics, mathematics, plant physiology, meteorology, anatomy, and plant breeding

Art

  • Requires practical skill even without deep scientific training

Business

  • Main objective is profit
