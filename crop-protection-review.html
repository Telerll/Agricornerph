Crop Protection Major Topics

1. Plant Pathology

Plant Pathology is the study of plant diseases, including their causes, symptoms, diagnosis, disease development, spread, resistance, and control.

Coverage

  • Plant diseases and diagnosis
  • Symptoms and signs
  • Viruses, viroids, bacteria, mollicutes, fungi, and nematodes
  • Disease cycle and epidemiology
  • Pathogenicity and host response
  • Disease resistance and control

2. Entomology and Pest Management

Entomology and Pest Management covers insects, pest organisms, pest identification, pest damage, integrated pest management, insecticides, pesticide application, and calibration.

Coverage

  • Rodents and mollusks as pests
  • Insect characteristics and external anatomy
  • Mouthparts, antennae, legs, wings, and abdomen
  • Insect classification, orders, metamorphosis, and reproduction
  • Concept of pest and insect pest control management
  • Integrated Pest Management or IPM
  • Insecticides
  • Pesticide calculation and calibration of application equipment

3. Weed Science

Weed Science focuses on weeds, their characteristics, classification, reproduction, dormancy, germination, crop-weed competition, allelopathy, weed management, and herbicides.

Coverage

  • Definition and characteristics of weeds
  • Classification of weeds
  • Weed reproduction and dissemination
  • Seed germination and seedling growth
  • Dormancy and breaking dormancy
  • Crop and weed competition
  • Allelopathy
  • Weed management methods
  • Herbicides and chemical weed control
