🌍 Part 10: Soil Management & Classification

Soil Management

  • Proper use, maintenance, and improvement of soil.
  • Aims to sustain productivity and prevent degradation.

Soil Fertility Management

  • Maintains or improves soil nutrients.
  • Includes fertilizers, organic matter, and crop rotation.

Soil Conservation

  • Protection of soil from erosion and degradation.
  • Methods: contour farming, terracing, strip cropping, windbreaks.

Soil Erosion

  • Removal of topsoil by water, wind, or human activity.
  • Reduces soil fertility.

Soil Degradation

  • Decline in soil quality due to misuse.
  • Causes: over-cultivation, deforestation, pollution.

Soil Salinity

  • Accumulation of salts in soil.
  • Reduces plant growth and water uptake.

Soil Acidity & Alkalinity

  • Acidity – low pH soil.
  • Alkalinity – high pH soil.

Soil Amendments

  • Materials added to improve soil.
  • Lime reduces acidity; organic matter improves structure.

Soil Classification

  • Grouping of soils based on characteristics.
  • Used for land use and agriculture planning.

Soil Taxonomy

  • Scientific system for classifying soils.

Soil Orders

Entisols

Very young soils with little development.

Inceptisols

Young soils with weak horizon development.

Alfisols

Moderately fertile soils with clay accumulation.

Ultisols

Strongly weathered, acidic soils.

Oxisols

Highly weathered tropical soils.

Mollisols

Dark, fertile grassland soils.

Vertisols

Clay soils that crack when dry.

Aridisols

Dry desert soils.

Spodosols

Acidic forest soils.

Histosols

Organic-rich soils.

Andisols

Volcanic ash soils.

Gelisols

Frozen soils with permafrost.

Land Capability Classification

  • Class I – most suitable
  • Class VIII – not suitable

Soil Survey

  • Study and mapping of soils.
  • Used for planning and agriculture.

Sustainable Soil Management

  • Maintains productivity for future generations.
  • Focus on conservation and environmental protection.

Summary

Soil Management → proper use of soil
Soil Classification → grouping of soil
Soil Taxonomy → scientific classification
