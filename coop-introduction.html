Definition of a Cooperative

The International Co-operative Alliance (ICA), established in 1895, is the oldest and largest worldwide federation of cooperative organizations. It represents over 90 countries and more than 750 million members globally.

ICA Definition

“A cooperative is an autonomous association of persons united voluntarily to meet their common economic, social, and cultural needs and aspirations through a jointly-owned and democratically-controlled enterprise.”

Alternative Definition

A cooperative is an organization formed by people with shared interests and problems aiming to improve their quality of life through collective action.

Core Values

  • Self-help
  • Self-responsibility
  • Democracy
  • Equality
  • Equity
  • Solidarity
