Introduction & Definition of Cooperatives

A cooperative is an autonomous association of persons united voluntarily to meet their common economic, social, and cultural needs and aspirations through a jointly-owned and democratically-controlled enterprise.

Cooperatives are organized to help members improve their income, reduce costs, and promote collective growth through shared resources and cooperation.

They operate based on principles of self-help, self-responsibility, democracy, equality, equity, and solidarity.
