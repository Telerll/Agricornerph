🫀 Part 2: Anatomy & Physiology

🔬 Cell & Cell Division

Cell – basic unit of life.

  • Nucleus – control center
  • Mitochondria – energy production
  • Ribosomes – protein synthesis
Mitosis = growth (2 cells)
Meiosis = reproduction (4 cells)

🦴 Skeletal System

Skeletal system – supports and protects the body.

  • Support and structure
  • Protects organs
  • Produces blood cells

💪 Muscular System

Muscular system – enables movement.

  • Skeletal – voluntary
  • Smooth – involuntary
  • Cardiac – heart muscle

🍽 Digestive System

Digestive system – breaks down food.

  • Mouth → Stomach → Intestines
  • Absorption occurs in small intestine

❤️ Circulatory System

Circulatory system – transports nutrients and oxygen.

  • Heart pumps blood
  • Blood carries oxygen

🌬 Respiratory System

Respiratory system – gas exchange.

  • Oxygen in
  • Carbon dioxide out

🚽 Excretory System

Excretory system – removes waste.

  • Kidneys filter blood

🧠 Nervous System

Nervous system – controls body activities.

  • Brain and spinal cord

🧪 Endocrine System

Endocrine system – produces hormones.

  • Pituitary – master gland
  • Thyroid – metabolism

🧬 Reproductive System

Reproductive system – produces offspring.

🧫 Lymphatic System

Lymphatic system – immunity and fluid balance.

🌡 Homeostasis

Homeostasis – maintaining stable internal environment.
