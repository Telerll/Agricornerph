Marketing Channels

Pathway through which agricultural products move from producers to consumers.

Marketing Channels

Marketing channels refer to the routes or pathways through which products move from producers to final consumers.

These channels involve different intermediaries who help in distribution.

Intermediaries or Resellers

These are individuals or organizations involved in the distribution process between producers and consumers.

Marketing / Distribution Flow

This refers to the movement of ownership and products from production to final consumption.

Importance of Marketing Channels

Ensures products reach consumers efficiently

Reduces gaps between producers and consumers

Helps in proper distribution of goods
