Biotechnology in Animal Breeding

Biotechnology in animal breeding is the application of scientific and laboratory techniques to improve animal reproduction, genetics, and production.

It uses biological tools to accelerate genetic improvement beyond conventional breeding.

Biotechnology improves breeding faster and more precisely.

Importance of Biotechnology

Biotechnology is important because it helps:

  • Accelerate genetic improvement
  • Improve reproductive efficiency
  • Multiply superior animals faster
  • Improve disease resistance
  • Support modern livestock production
Biotechnology increases speed and precision of breeding.

Major Biotechnologies in Animal Breeding

  • Artificial Insemination (AI)
  • Estrus Synchronization
  • Embryo Transfer (ET)
  • Multiple Ovulation Embryo Transfer (MOET)
  • In Vitro Fertilization (IVF)
  • Sexed Semen
  • Cloning
  • Marker-Assisted Selection (MAS)
  • Genomic Selection
